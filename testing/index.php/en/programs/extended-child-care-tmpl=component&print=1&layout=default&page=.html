Extended Child Care

Recognizing the varied schedules of working parents, the International Academy provides safe, secure and consistent extended day care for IAAS students as well as children attending other private or public schools.  The before-school program operates from 7:00-9:00 am and includes breakfast time followed by energy-conserving activities.  The after-school program operates from 3:00-6:00 pm and includes snack time followed by age-appropriate activities. Limited transportation is available to and from localized areas.  Children have ample opportunity to engage in both teacher-directed and child-initiated experiences, enabling them to explore the world they live in as they read, play indoors and outdoors, investigate science and join in music and art activities.  Optional private music and language classes are available upon request.
