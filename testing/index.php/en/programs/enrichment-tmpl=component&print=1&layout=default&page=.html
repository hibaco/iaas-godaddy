Enrichment

At IAAS, we believe that learning new skills is as fun as it is educational.  We offer a wide variety of early afternoon, after-school, evening, and weekend experiences to appeal to students of all ages.

Individual and group lessons:

  • instrumental music and theory are available for voice, piano, violin, and cello
  • motion classes for ballet, jazz, and stretch

Group lessons:

  • 3 Steps to a Great Kid.   We are proud to partner with the Chamberlain School of Self Defense to offer this martial arts character development program.  This unique approach teaches responsibility and accountability at home, in school, and within the dojo.
  • Women Empowered teaches self-defense and confidence to women of all ages. 
  • Broadway Kids offers a total experience that includes all aspects of producing a musical play, such as singing, dancing, costume design, and prop and set creation. 
  • Japanese, Spanish and Mandarin classes.  Kindly inform us of other world language classes we ought to be adding to our program.
