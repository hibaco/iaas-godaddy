Our Location

The International Academy is located in the heart of Bergen County's Northern Valley, midway between the George Washington and Tappan Zee Bridges.  We share a parking lot with the Closter Public Library and Ferrell's Woods, which is adjoined to the Belskie Art Museum.  Students come from Bergen, Morris, Essex, Hudson and Rockland Counties.  The quality and value of the International Academy attracts families within a 30-mile radius of the school.  Bus/van service is provided from the Fort Lee / Edgewater area.  Kindly inform us if there are other routes you would like us to consider.
