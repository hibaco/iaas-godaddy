Instituto Cervantes is now at IAAS in Closter, NJ!



International Academy of Arts and Sciences (IAAS) is proud to be the New Jersey home of Instituto Cervantes New York, a revered cultural and language institution based in Manhattan.

Spanish Language Courses

IAAS will hold Instituto Cervantes Spanish language classes at their convenient Closter, NJ location. Students taking classes at the NJ location receive a one-year membership to Instituto Cervantes in New York City, which includes access to conferences, converts, film screenings and social events as well as discounts to restaurants, stores, cultural events and theaters.

About ICNY

The Instituto Cervantes of Spain was founded in 1991 with the aim of promoting the Spanish language and disseminating the cultures of Spain and all the Latin American countries. The non-profit, public foundation is represented worldwide with 70 institutions in more than 40 countries.
