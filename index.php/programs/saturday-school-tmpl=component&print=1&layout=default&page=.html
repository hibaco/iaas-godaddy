Saturday School

The Asunaro Language School is a full Japanese immersion program for children ages 4 to 6.  The program takes place every Saturday morning from 9:00-11:30 during the academic year.  The small teacher-student ratio creates a close-knit environment of a seminar where children are given many opportunities to participate and ask questions.

In keeping with the philosophy of IAAS, the program lets “kids be kids”-- jumping rope to popular rhymes; singing; reading inspiring books-- and sets the tone for a beautiful, enlightening weekend.
