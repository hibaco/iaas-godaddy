PTA & IAAS Volunteers

IAAS volunteers come from a wide range of backgrounds. Some are family members of students; some work in music and the arts; and some are educators from the neighborhood.

IAAS’ Parent Teacher Association is an open forum that invites all parents and guardians to assist one another and the school in the education and care of our children.  We gather once each month to discuss upcoming events such as coordinating a parent/guardian led Teacher Appreciation Lunch; indoor activities in winter; and fundraising with a school-wide yard sale and bake sale.  The director joins the conversation to talk about the school's academic plans, curriculum goals, and issues pertinent to parents and children.  Little ones are always welcome to join and play.

The association dues are $10/ year and are donated to the school for the additional administrative expenses needed to support the PTA's activities.   

We look forward to seeing you!
