Our Philosophy

The International Academy of Arts and Sciences is guided by the belief that children learn by doing.

By following a model of experiential education, our students are immersed in a nurturing and supportive environment where our accomplished teachers provide the freedom to think critically and work creatively through a curriculum that engages in:

  • dialogue in both native and non-native languages (English, Japanese, Spanish; Chinese coming soon)
  • music on a daily basis
  • creative movement through stretch, dance and karate
  • “travel” to different countries with their very own school-issued passport
  • a sustainable lifestyle that incorporates the principle of “reduce, reuse, recycle”
  • hands-on scientific experiments that explore the cycles of the natural world
  • song and dance in confidence-building performances with friends and teachers
