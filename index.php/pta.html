SUPPORT US

Your contributions to the International Academy of Arts and Sciences are now tax-deductible! IAAS has obtained its 501(c)(3) status, organizing specifically to support capital campaigns and improvements, programs, and services for the overall educational advancement of the school. The IAAS Foundation will also work towards the development of a model "We're all Included" music workshop for interested schools to emulate a creative anti-bullying initiative.

Nearly one thousand corporations have a “matching gift program,” enabling you to double your contribution to IAAS. Among these companies are Verizon, Merck, Goldman Sachs, Nissan, Sony and hundreds more. Kindly check this list before sending your contribution.

We will issue you a letter at the end of the year so that you can be sure to deduct your donation on your tax returns.

There are many ways that you can support IAAS and we depend on support from individuals, community partners and businesses to continue to expand the number of students we reach and the quality of the education that they receive!

PTA & IAAS Volunteers

IAAS volunteers come from a wide range of backgrounds. Some are family members of students; some work in music and the arts; and some are educators from the neighborhood.

IAAS’ Parent Teacher Association is an open forum that invites all parents and guardians to assist one another and the school in the education and care of our children. We gather once each month to discuss upcoming events such as coordinating a parent/guardian led Teacher Appreciation Lunch; indoor activities in winter; and fundraising with a school-wide yard sale and bake sale. The director joins the conversation to talk about the school's academic plans, curriculum goals, and issues pertinent to parents and children. Little ones are always welcome to join and play.

The association dues are $10/ year and are donated to the school for the additional administrative expenses needed to support the PTA's activities.

We look forward to seeing you!
