Mission Statement

We believe that each and every child should start their education with an exceptional learning experience that is fun, memorable, and opens the mind to the diversity we find in nature and the world.  Out of respect and appreciation for each child's unlimited potential to learn, our school seeks to nurture the gifts of language, art, music, literature, movement and science to establish a strong foundation for a lifetime of joyful exploration.
