Extended Day Child Care

Two and a Half through Six Year Olds


Recognizing the varied schedules of working parents, IAAS provides safe,
secure and consistent extended day care for students of the Academy as well
as children attending other private or public schools. The before school
program operates from 7:00-9:00 am and includes breakfast time followed by
energy-conserving activities. The after school program operates from 3:00-
6:00 pm and includes snack time followed by age-appropriate activities. Limited
transportation is available to and from localized areas. Children have ample
opportunity to engage in both teacher-directed and child-initiated experiences,
enabling them to explore the world they live in as they read, play indoors and
outdoors, investigate science and join in music and art activities. Optional
private music classes are also available upon request.
